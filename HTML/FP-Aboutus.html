   Our founder, Aleph, knows what a hassle it is to cite sources properly. This is why he designed a program that could format the entire syntax for citing sources using just the URL. As a separate service, our software quickly and easily checks your papers for bad and awkward grammar.

   After creating a simple version during college in 2021, Aleph spent more time polishing and refining his program into what you see today. Aleph would go on to add a plagurism checker and even a grammar checker. Now, Aleph would like to share the same convience without all the hassle.
